Circus Citrusis a contemporary performance art collective. Our mission is to bring unexpected lemonade into the ordinary December darkness. We only perform when it's dark outside, illuminated by the glow of street lights. We lurk in the shadows for a joyful surprise. When life brings you lemons, we bring you lemonade. Cliché as it sounds.
